Title:: Are You My Mother?
Author:: P.D. Eastman
Description:: A baby bird goes in search of his mother in this hilarious Beginner Book edited by Dr. Seuss. When a mother bird's egg starts to jump, she hurries off to make sure she has something for her little one to eat. But as soon as she's gone, out pops the baby bird. He immediately sets off to find his mother, but not knowing what she looks like makes it a challenge. 
 
 The little hatchling is determined to find his mother, even after meeting a kitten, a hen, a dog, and a Snort.
