Author:: Wilbur Smith
Description:: On the pretext of making a film for his mother's environmental protection work, Jack wrangles an expedition to the Arctic with his friends Amelia and Xander and cousin Caleb. Is the renew able energy investment company really as environmentally responsible as it claims to be? Can Jack really trust his mother's friend Jonny Armfield to come to their help, or is he part of the problem? With sensitive themes and fast-paced action this audiobook is for listeners aged 10 and up.
