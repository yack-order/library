Title:: Brown Bear & Friends
Author:: Bill Martin Jr.
Description:: Bill Martin Jr and Eric Carle's classic Bear books have been a hit with children for many generations and have sold more than 11 million copies. This wonderful compilation brings together the beloved first three Bear books - Brown Bear, Brown Bear, What Do You See?; Polar Bear, Polar Bear, What Do You Hear?; and Panda Bear, Panda Bear, What Do You See? - and includes the newest addition, Baby Bear, Baby Bear, What Do You See?. The compilation also features sections read in Spanish.

The stories are read with warmth and humor by Academy Award-winning actress and mother Gwyneth Paltrow. This audio book includes a bonus interview with the author.
