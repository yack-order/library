Title:: Kids Ask Dr. Friendtastic
Author:: Eileen Kennedy-Moore, PhD
Description:: Kids Ask Dr. Friendtastic is a weekly, five-minute podcast for kids about making and keeping friends. Each episode features an audio recording of a kid's question about friendship, plus a practical and thought-provoking answer from Eileen Kennedy-Moore, PhD (aka "Dr. Friendtastic"), an author and clinical psychologist based in Princeton, NJ. 

To submit a question, https://DrFriendtastic.com/submit.
