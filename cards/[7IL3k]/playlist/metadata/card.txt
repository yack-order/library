Description:: The Folk of the Faraway Tree is the third magical story in the Faraway Tree series by the world’s best-loved children’s author, Enid Blyton.

Join them and their friends Moonface, Saucepan Man and Silky the fairy as they discover which new land is at the top of the Faraway Tree.
