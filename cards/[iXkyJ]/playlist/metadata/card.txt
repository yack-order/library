Author:: The Wonderling featuring Rie Sinclair
Description:: The Wonder Club is a set of 12 STEAM-based nursery rhymes about the numbers 0 to 11, offering an exploration of early learning concepts through pattern-recognition, and reinforcing a curiosity essential for critical thinking. These short, catchy melodies are enjoyable between parent and child, bridging a gap between education and entertainment, while supporting adaptable problem-solving and counting. The Wonder Club aims to create a meaningful impact on children's development by combining art, science, and philosophy to foster wonder and awe. Excellent for gestalt learners! Enjoy The Wonder Club as a Musical Picture Book with negative space illustrations. Recorded and produced on analogue tape with toy instruments, synths and field recordings by Emmy-Nominated songwriter Rie Sinclair.
