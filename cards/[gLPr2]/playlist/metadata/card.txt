Title:: Ladybird Stories for Easter
Author:: Ladybird
Description:: Brought to you by Ladybird.  

A heart-warming, and engaging, collection of original audio stories celebrating Easter.  In this collection we follow different families as they spend Easter together. From church services and sacred traditions to egg hunts and family times, these are fun and informative stories for 3-7 year olds.  

These stories are perfect for young children celebrating Easter with their friends and family, or wanting to learn about different Christian traditions.  These stories are aimed at children aged three to six for family listening, learning, and the development of young minds.  

Other audiobooks in the Ladybird Audio Original series include Ladybird Magical Stories, Ladybird Funny Nativity Play and the Ladybird Audio Adventures series.
