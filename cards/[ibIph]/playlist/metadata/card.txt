Title:: Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood: Fun in the Sun
Author:: Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood
Description:: Join Daniel Tiger, Katerina Kittycat, Prince Wednesday, Miss Elaina, O the Owl, and all your favorite Neighborhood pals for some sunshine playtime! Daniel and his friends share their big feelings at the lemonade stand, on the playground, and while riding their bikes. They also learn the importance of staying safe at the beach and at the pool, and even learn a new, exciting thing about their beloved Trolley!

This collection of short stories is perfect for summertime, or any time you just want to hear a sunny story! Each track features audio directly from the PBS KIDS series, “Daniel Tiger’s Neighborhood,” inspired by the classic, “Mister Rogers’ Neighborhood.”

Stories include:
1. Safety at the Beach
2. The Lemonade Stand
3. Daniel’s First Swim Class
4. Daniel’s Bicycle
5. The Big Slide
6. Daniel Follows the Rules at the Pool
7. Daniel’s Grr-ific Grandpere
8. Daniel’s Nature Walk
9. Something New About Trolley
